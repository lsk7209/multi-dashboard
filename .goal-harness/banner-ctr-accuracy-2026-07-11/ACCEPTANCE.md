# Acceptance

- [ ] Qualified impression and click events share an anonymous session token.
- [ ] Automated traffic is excluded from qualified metrics.
- [ ] Qualified CTR is computed from qualified unique sessions, not raw endpoint calls.
- [ ] Fewer than 100 qualified impressions is shown as insufficient, not ranked.
- [ ] All managed installation paths are upgraded and verified.
- [ ] Unit, integration, build, and production smoke evidence is recorded.
